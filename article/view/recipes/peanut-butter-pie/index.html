Meme's Decadent Peanut Butter Pie

1 min · Jay Little

Ingredients List#

  • 8 ounces of Cream Cheese

  • 1 cup of Peanut Butter

    Creamy, not that Chunky Garbage

  • 16 ounces of Cool Whip

  • 1 1/2 cups of Powdered Sugar

  • Pre-made Graham Cracker or Oreo Pie Crust

How to Make#

  1. Soften Cream Cheese (leave it in room temperature for a bit).

    This makes it easier to complete the following step

  2. Mix Cream Cheese, Peanut Butter and Cool Whip until fluffy

    Pro Tip: Do it with a real Kitchen-Aid mixer, anything else is just you lying to yourself

  3. Add Powdered Sugar and Mix Well

  4. Pour Into Crust

  5. Chill

Notes#

Makes two pies. This recipe is from Annette’s dearly departed mother and one of many ways in which we remember her.
