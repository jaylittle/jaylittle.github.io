Pa Little's Tuna Casserole

1 min · Jay Little

Ingredients List#

  • 3 Cups Uncooked Elbow Macaroni

  • 1 Can (6 oz) Tuna

  • 1 Can (10.5 oz) Cream of Mushroom Soup

  • 1 Soup Can of Milk

    Non-Dairy milks such as Almond, Oat or Soy also work and we prefer them

  • 6 oz Bag Potato Chips Crushed

  • Black Pepper

How to Make#

  1. Boil a bowl/pot of water

  2. Add uncooked macaroni to boiling water

  3. Boil for 8 minutes until done

  4. Drain macaroni

  5. Mix Mushroom soup, 1 soup can of milk, and drained tuna in a casserole dish

  6. Mix well

  7. Add macaroni

  8. Sprinkle with crushed potato chips and sprinkle with pepper.

  9. Microwave on high for 15 minutes.

Notes#

My father originally created this when he was a broke college student in California. He made it at least once a month when we were kids. So me and my siblings all love it to this day. It’s cheap, easy and quick to make.

Pro tip: It’s even better when you reheat the leftovers and eat it that way!
